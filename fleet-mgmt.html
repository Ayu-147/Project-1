Frequently asked questions

  • What is Uber’s Supplier Portal?

    Supplier Portal is a web-based fleet management tool that lets individuals, entrepreneurs, and business owners easily manage their fleet, drivers, and earnings on the Uber platform, with no cost.

    Go to Supplier Portal
  • Do I need a minimum number of vehicles to get started?

    You can start with one vehicle. There is no limit to the number of vehicles you can list for rent.

  • How can I create an Uber supplier account?

    To sign up for an Uber supplier account, click here. Then upload the required documents to complete the registration of your fleet. Once registration is complete, you can use Uber’s Supplier Portal to find drivers and manage your vehicles.

  • How can I get support?

    When you’re part of Uber’s community, you are never alone. Use the Help button in our Supplier Portal to get support.

  • How do I add my vehicles to Uber?

    First you’ll need to sign up as a supplier here. Then upload the required documents to complete the registration of your fleet. Once registration is complete, you can use Uber’s Supplier Portal to add your vehicles.
